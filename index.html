How DeVPN Works

Our decentralized architecture ensures privacy, security, and performance.

1

Connect to DeVPN

Download the DeVPN application, create an account, and connect with a single click. You can pay for the service using DVPN tokens or traditional payment methods.

2

Smart Node Selection

Our algorithm automatically selects the optimal nodes from our decentralized network based on your location, desired server location, and current network conditions.

3

Multi-Hop Routing

Your traffic is routed through multiple nodes in our network, each only knowing the previous and next hop, making it impossible to track your online activity.

4

Blockchain Verification

Transactions between users and node operators are verified on the blockchain, ensuring transparency and fair compensation for bandwidth providers.
